# Demo Queries

Use these questions after indexing a small set of papers about retrieval-augmented generation, LLM evaluation, knowledge graph question answering, or data science automation.

1. What problem does retrieval-augmented generation try to solve?
2. Which retrieval method is used in the indexed papers?
3. What datasets or benchmarks are mentioned for evaluating LLM systems?
4. What limitations do the papers report about retrieval quality?
5. How do the papers handle evidence or citations in generated answers?
6. What are the main differences between GraphRAG and standard vector RAG?
7. Which paper discusses hallucination or unsupported claims?
8. What evaluation metrics are used for retrieval performance?
9. What are potential applications of the proposed methods?
10. Which future work directions are suggested by the papers?
